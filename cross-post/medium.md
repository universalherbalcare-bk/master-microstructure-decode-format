**Abstract:** A 75-dimension extraction protocol (55 subject-decomposition + 20 action-layer) paired with an 8-tier relational escalation ladder, formatted for dual use: a standalone research/analysis methodology, and a droppable skill definition for generative-AI tooling. Forces non-obvious structure out of any subject or creation task, with explicit rules against manufactured false depth.

A description says what something is called. A decode says what it's made of, what it rests on, who it serves, and what it hides. This protocol turns that principle into 55 exact per-dimension instructions covering everything from Formulas and Method to Power Dynamics, Evidence Base, and Time Sensitivity — plus 20 action-layer dimensions that activate for research, build, develop, generate, create, find, or decode work.

Layered on top is an 8-tier relational depth ladder — because hidden structure lives in the connections between things, not in their definitions. It runs from a basic dependency map through feedback-loop detection, rigorous correlation-vs-causation testing, cross-domain pattern transfer, a hidden/suppressed-relations check, up to network-level leverage-point synthesis. Escalation stops the moment a tier stops adding anything real; a simple subject honestly capping at Tier 2 is the correct result, not a shortfall.
