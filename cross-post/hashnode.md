## Abstract

A 75-dimension extraction protocol (55 subject-decomposition + 20 action-layer) paired with an 8-tier relational escalation ladder, formatted for dual use: a standalone research/analysis methodology, and a droppable skill definition for generative-AI tooling. It forces non-obvious structure out of any subject or creation task, with explicit rules against manufactured false depth.

## The core idea

A description says what something is called. A decode says what it's made of, what it rests on, who it serves, and what it hides. This protocol operationalizes that distinction into 55 exact per-dimension instructions (Point, Method, Logic, Failure Modes, Power Dynamics, Evidence Base, and 49 more), plus 20 action-layer dimensions that kick in whenever the task is research, build, develop, generate, create, find, or decode rather than pure analysis.

## The relational ladder

Hidden structure lives in the connections, not the definitions. The protocol escalates through 8 tiers — from a basic 1-hop dependency map (Tier 0) through feedback-loop detection, correlation-vs-causation testing, cross-domain isomorphic mapping, a hidden/suppressed-relations silence test, up to network-level leverage-point synthesis (Tier 7). It stops the moment a tier adds nothing new — reaching Tier 7 isn't mandatory, and honestly reporting a lower ceiling is the rigorous outcome.
