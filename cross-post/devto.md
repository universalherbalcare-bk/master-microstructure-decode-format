## What it is

Most "be thorough" frameworks give you a handful of lenses. This one gives 75 non-redundant, independently actionable dimensions — 55 for decomposing anything that already exists, plus 20 action-layer dimensions specifically for research, build, develop, generate, create, find, or decode work — paired with an explicit depth-escalation ladder (Tier 0 → Tier 7) and a stopping rule against manufactured false complexity.

## Quick invocation

```
DECODE: <subject>
MODE: analyze | research | build | develop | generate | create | find | decode-literal
```

`analyze` runs the 55-dimension subject decomposition only. Any other MODE adds the 20 action-layer dimensions on top.

## Why it's different

- **Explicit stopping rule.** Escalate tier by tier through the relational ladder; stop the moment a tier adds no new load-bearing connection. Reaching Tier 7 (network-level leverage-point synthesis) is not mandatory — a simple subject capping at Tier 2 and saying so honestly *is* the rigorous result.
- **Correlation never silently becomes causation.** Every relational finding runs a three-part causation test (mechanism, confounding, timing); fail all three and it's tagged `[CORRELATION-ONLY]` permanently.
- **Built for both humans and agents.** The file ships with skill-style frontmatter (`name`, `description`, trigger phrases) so it drops directly into a generative-AI skills directory.
